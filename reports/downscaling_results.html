Downscaling Results and Analysis

Author

David LeBauer

Published

April 6, 2026

Overview

Here we present results of downscaling SIPNET model outputs from ~200 anchor sites to ~132,000 annual cropland fields across 57 California counties. Results include county-level carbon stocks, field-level density maps, and mixed baseline-plus-scenario-difference comparisons for soil carbon, aboveground biomass, and GHG fluxes (N₂O, CH₄).

Not validated, for illustrative purposes only.

For detailed information about methods and workflow, see the Workflow Documentation.
